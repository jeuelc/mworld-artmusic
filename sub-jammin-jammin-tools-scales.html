Scales

 

Scales are groups of notes that can be used to make melodies. Certain orders of notes can make different-sounding scales. Using notes from those scales, you can make melodies, or phrases that suit the style of the song youre playing.

Scales are kind of like languages. If you know the right words (notes) in the language (scale), you can use them to make sentences (phrases). Shuffle those words around, or use different words, and you have a different sentence or phrase.

There are a few main types of scales, including the major, minor, the minor pentatonic (or just pentatonic for short) and blues. Each one has a different sound, and can be used to make different types of phrases. Some music styles use mostly one or two scales, but skilled musicians often use lots of them when they are jamming. You might know a few of them yourself!
