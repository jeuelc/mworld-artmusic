Big Noise!

 

Drums are great fun to play together and produce an incredible sound, so you will often find percussion played in large groups, or ensembles. Sometimes these ensembles have just a few people, like a Brazilian Samba group, and sometimes they are very large, like an Indonesian Gamelan Orchestra.

These groups often play music that is organised and composed so that each musician plays a special part. They usually feature a range of percussion instruments of all shapes and sizes that make different sounds, including some played with sticks and beaters and some with the hands.

While there are traditional ensembles like the taiko drumming ensembles in Japan or drumming groups of Sri Lanka, there are also many new ensembles that mix together different percussion instruments from many countries, making new multicultural music!
