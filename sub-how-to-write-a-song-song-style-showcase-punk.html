Punk was a movement that happened in the early 1980s, and brought music that combined the aggression of rock with protest and rebellion themes. It saw a lot of mohawk hairstyles, leather clothes and safety pins as punks spoke out against traditional ways of acting in society they wanted the right to do anything they wanted to. Some bands from this time include The Clash, The Ramones and the Sex Pistols.

Punk is characterised by a different take on normal instrument playing, often playing in 'wrong' or uncommon ways. Punk songs are often short with strong messages and are a played in a loud, aggressive and 'hard edged' style.
