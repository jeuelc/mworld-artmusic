What else is missing from your attempt to capture reality?

What about your feelings when you were making the drawing? How can you communicate these?

What mood were you in and what were you thinking?

Is it possible to communicate these things?

The artist Eduard Munch, for example, uses intense lines, colour and shapes to indicate anxiety, as in his famous work The Scream, but also in his other lesser known works such as Vampire.

What other things are missing from your drawing that you would like to add? This is where you can add your own voice, ideas and seeing to your drawing.
