Jammin' in the Style Of...

There are some styles where people expect to have improvised jams, such as jazz music. Then there are others, such as classical music, where jamming isnt as common but this doesnt prevent a classical musician jamming on a contemporary piece. Musicians can jam in all sorts of styles of music! Some even combine different styles in their jams to make new and interesting types of music.

So what makes one style different from another? The short answer is rhythm - the length of time each beat and note is played for. Different rhythms will make listeners and musicians feel different sorts of energy and emotion, and they help separate one style from another.

Lets explore a few important styles of music, and discover where you might find the styles used by people when jamming.
