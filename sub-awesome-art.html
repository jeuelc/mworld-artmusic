Awesome Art

There is an enormous amount of art all around the world and there is always new art being created.

Art comes in many different forms drawing, painting, sculpture, body and performance art, print making, photography, jewellery, video, installation, and more!

Artists are inspired by many different things their view of the world, places they like, people they love, fear, loss, happiness, frustration, and more.

Artists' work is influenced by time, place and culture and what we think of their work is influenced by our time, place and culture!
