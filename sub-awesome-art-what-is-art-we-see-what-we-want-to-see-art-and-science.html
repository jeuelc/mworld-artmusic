Art and science

An artist expresses their sense of the truth about the world they live in. They create art to reveal new ways of looking at the world for them and for all of us to experience.

In this way artists are a bit like scientists - they seek to understand the world. But they do it in very different ways, and also ask how it feels to see and experience the world.

Many artists use science and technology to make their art and to inspire their art. It might seem that art and science are very different ways of looking at the world, but actually there is much in common too. Science has had a huge impact on our world, but so too has art and the ideas it expresses.
