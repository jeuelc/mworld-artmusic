Metal music, also called heavy metal, is one of the many 'sub-genres' of rock music, made up of extreme rhythms and aggressive energy. It's usually louder, faster and 'harder' than other styles of rock.

Metal is dominated by heavy beats, massive and loud sounds from instruments like electric guitars and drums, and singing styles that focus more on power than melody and even lyrics. Metal songs are often written with very high, shrill singing styles. It is often a dark style of music, with songs that share angry or frustrated feelings, or tell fantasy-type stories. Some different metal bands to listen to are Metallica, Van Halen, and Lacuna Coil.
