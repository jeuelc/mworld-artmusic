Have you ever tried to really listen to the bass notes in a song you like? Most people don't notice the deep notes in music but if you do, you realise they give structure to the music. It's the same with drawing negative space the space that isnt your object.

In your next drawing you will focus on the spaces around the object. This is called drawing the negative space. It reverses the thing we tend to focus on first the object. (or in drawing language- the figure). Instead, you will draw the spaces between and around the objects.
