Tones

You are going to work with just four tones for your tone drawing.

You will need some white paper. Next, you need to have four tones. You can make a little grouping of these tones on the side of your paper. A black, a dark grey, a light grey and the white of the paper for the highlights.

In order to get this you could use three different greylead pencils (a 4B for the black, a 2B for the dark grey and a B for the light grey) or coloured pencils, or coloured textas.
