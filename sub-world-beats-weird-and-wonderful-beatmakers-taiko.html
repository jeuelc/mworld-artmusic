Taiko

 

The o-daiko drum is the biggest drum in Japanese taiko drumming ensembles. The o-daiko is played with big sticks held above the head in a martial arts style. Many o-daiko drums are between two and three metres across the skin, and can weigh over three tonnes! Thats heavier than 50 people put together!

Many o-daikos are made from a single piece of wood from trees that are over 1000 years old.
