Chords

 

Chords are played by instruments such as the guitar, piano or keyboard, and are responsible for giving songs their overall colour and mood. They support and highlight the melody, giving it a foundation to be sung over and making it more noticeable.

There are two main types of chords: major chords and minor chords. Major chords are strong and 'happy sounding' while minor chords are darker and often sound 'sad'. A song made up of all major chords tends to sound brighter and more upbeat, whereas all minor chords might make you feel quite upset! Most songs use a combination of both, but the type of chord they use the most often sets the overall colour of the song.

Playing the same chord progressions in different musical styles can also make the same song sound completely different.
