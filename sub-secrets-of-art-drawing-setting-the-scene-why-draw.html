Why Draw?

 
click here

Have you ever watched newborn babies look at the world around them? They look with incredible intensity at what they see because they dont know what they are looking at.

In a way, we forget to look as we grow up and fix labels or ideas to what we know. Really, we stop perceiving and most of the experience we have of the world is based on memory. We already know what this or that thing is, so we stop looking properly. We see what we know.

The first great secret of drawing is to unlearn what you know about what you are drawing! This is so you can see it again as if for the first time. Artists look with great intensity at the world around them to see the unexpected and look at the overlooked.
