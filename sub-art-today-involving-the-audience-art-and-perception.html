Art and Perception

Icelandic artist Olafur Eliasson makes installations about how we see the world. His works have titles like 'Your Vision or Your Emotional Future' to highlight that the work is about the viewer's way of seeing. Eliasson's work often refers to science but not necessarily in a scientific way. He looks at how you experience the world of light, sound, temperature, and time through your bodily senses.

Eliasson's works focus on the viewer and encourage the viewer to be involved. Eliasson's work suggests that in many ways we create the world at the same time as we experience it.

The Belgian artist Carsten Holler makes works that are about our bodily experience of art. One of his projects is to install long, spiralling slides in large public galleries.
