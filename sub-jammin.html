Jammin'

When bands and musicians play music, they might practise and perform songs that are already written. But sometimes, musicians like to make up music just for the fun of it. So, they jam!

'Jamming' is when musicians come together to play music that hasnt been prepared, arranged or written yet. Its a fun way to play and make up new music with other people, and can also be a great way to practise an instrument.

Jamming is for everyone too - not just advanced musicians. Anyone who can make sound with an instrument, their voice, or just something to hit can jam. You can jam without reading music or even knowing any songs. All you need is a few ideas, some tools, your ears, and someone to jam with.

Let's explore how people jam, and learn some tools that you can use to jam with your friends!
