Rock 'n' Roll music appeared in the 1940s and 1950s and electrified the song! It evolved from blues and folk music. Using electric guitars and drums, it focused on heavy rhythms that made you want to stamp your feet. Rock and roll music was cutting edge when it started, being played louder, faster and less traditionally than other styles. Early songs described events and conflicts that teenagers and young adults could relate to, like relationships, school, cars, clothes and being 'cool'.

Rock and roll made people want to dance and was filled with energy. Today, it's more commonly just called Rock music, with lot of smaller, variation styles or 'sub genres'. It's still loud, and has even more energy than before! There are so many different styles, but some famous artists include Elvis Presley, The Beatles, and ACDC.
