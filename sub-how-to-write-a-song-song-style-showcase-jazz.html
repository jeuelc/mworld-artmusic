Jazz music evolved from blues music and European classical music at the beginning of the 20th century, and has continued to evolve. Typical jazz songs have a swinging beat, more complex chord progressions, and are written to leave lots of space for improvisation that is, melodies made up on the spot! Some great historical jazz writers include Nina Simone, Ella Fitzgerald and Duke Ellington.

The lyrics of jazz often tell stories of love, heartbreak and loss, and the melodies are incredibly strong and memorable. People have lots of different ideas about what jazz music is, and there are lots of different types, including traditional, bebop and experimental art jazz.
