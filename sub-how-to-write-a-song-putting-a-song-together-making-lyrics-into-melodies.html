Making Lyrics into Melodies

 

When you have some lyrics, you'll want to try singing them with a melody. You wont need to read or write music though just use your ears!

Listen to a chord bed and try to hum or sing a melody along with it. You can then try to sing the lyrics using that melody.

If youre having trouble fitting all the words in, look at the syllables in each word and line of lyrics - 'po-e-try', 'syll-a-bles', 'ly-rics'. They can help find the rhythm and set your lyrics to the melody. It can help to have a similar number of syllables in each line. Try speaking them against the chords, and count how many there are. There might be too many. Could you use a different word or synonym, or take another word out?
