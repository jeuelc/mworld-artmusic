Finding the Form and Order

Not all songs need to be in Verse-Chorus-Verse-Chorus form. Some songs have lots of verses, and only a single line for a chorus. Others have very strong choruses, and dont really have many verses. Some have a two line bridge in the middle. Its up to you to decide whats best for your song! You could start with a chorus to introduce the song then use the verses to fill in the gaps in the story. Or you could just write verses and have a little whistling melody for the chorus.

The golden rule is to make sure theres enough repetition to make your song memorable. The last line of a verse might be the same each time, or the chorus might have a very catchy melody. If you find you've written a chorus you can't stop singing, you just might have the makings of a really great song!
