Creating the Beat

 

So, what is rhythm?

Rhythm is the division of time, or the ordering and arrangement of time. Just like a clock divides up time into hours, minutes and seconds, rhythm in music divides sound into smaller or larger chunks.

If you divide up the time into small pieces and play notes or beats closer together, the music will be fast and give you a feeling of rushing along. If you divide the time into large parts and play beats farther apart, the music will be slow and feel relaxed.
