Percussion is used in lots of different ways. Learning about some of these will help you get a better idea of the nature of drumming.

Many cultures around the world use drums in their traditional ceremonies. In China, percussion groups perform with a dancing lion to celebrate important events like the new year. In fact, people often dance to the sound of drums, like Egyptian belly dancers.

Drums are sometimes played in a community gathering where everybody is involved, like a group of West African drummers. Other times, you might listen to solo drummers perform in big concert halls, like a tabla player or a drum set player.

Did you know that sometimes drums are even used in battle? In many places around the world, including Europe, America and Japan, drums were used to march troops into war and to communicate orders to the troops.
