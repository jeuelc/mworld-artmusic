Space Masters

Looking at the work of artists who have attempted these things before is a great thing to do. You see some people have devoted their whole working lives to dealing with these problems.

Georgio Morandi was one such artist. Morandi devoted years to drawing and painting a series of small objects over and over again. Look at these examples of his work. You can see that the spaces between the objects are emphasised just as much as the objects themselves. The whole image seems to jostle and vibrate.
