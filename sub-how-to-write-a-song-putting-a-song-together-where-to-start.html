Where to Start

 

Some songs are written very quickly. You might have an idea of a story to tell, or feel something very strongly. But what if you dont know what to write about?

There are lots of different tools you can use to find ideas for songs. You could write about something that happened to you or a friend. You could imagine you are in a fairy tale and write about being trapped in a tower, waiting to be rescued. You could look at a picture of a landscape or painting and write about how it makes you feel. You could write about the weather, school, or your family. You could even find ideas from random words.

Great songs are often written with a clear idea from the start. If you know what your song is about, there's a good chance the listener will know too.
