World Beats

Welcome to the world of drums and percussion, home to things that go "Bing", "Bang" and "BOOOM"!

Inside the world of percussion you will find an amazing collection of drums and percussion instruments from around the world. They come in many different shapes and sizes, and produce an incredible array of different sounds.

Let's go on an exciting journey around the world to find different types of amazing drums and see where they come from. You'll see lots of drummers playing together in tribes and on stage, as well as amazing solo masters.

We'll also look back through time to see where their traditions were born, and see how those same drums are played throughout the world today. Let's get playing!
