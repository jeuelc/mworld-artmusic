Investigating our world

Modern artists have created many new types of art. There's constructivism, cubism, surrealism, abstract expressionism, pop art, minimalism, conceptual art, environmental art, performance art, postmodernism and many more.

You can think of each of these approaches as a grouping of artists wanting to investigate the world in a new and different way.

These new ways of doing art are more than just new styles. They show how the artists have responded to issues they are grappling with. Some of these issues are about art itself. Some are about protest and desire for social change. Some are about the natural world and the environment.

Let's explore the world of art today and some of these themes that artists are grappling with.
