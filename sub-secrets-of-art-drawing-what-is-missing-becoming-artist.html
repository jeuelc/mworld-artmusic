Becoming an Artist

You can do amazing things with drawing. Just follow these guidelines and you'll soon be excellent at drawing! But you do need to draw every day if you want to become really good.

Being a good artist these days involves much more than being good at drawing. The sorts of questions we have asked - like what else is missing from the work - have led artists to extend art into many different forms in the past 100 years. But drawing is a great place to start!

A good place to begin is by buying a journal, for example a thick-covered book with many blank pages of strong paper. Into this can go your drawings, your thoughts about the world, your observations and your dreams.

It's good to also start looking at art. Not just art on the internet, but art in galleries. You can learn so much from what other artists do. Take your journal with you and write about and draw what you see - and make sure you have fun!
