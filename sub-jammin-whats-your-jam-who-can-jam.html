Who Can Jam?

 
click here

Jamming is not just for expert musicians - anyone who has an instrument, or a way to make sound can do it. You can even sing, click your fingers, or just hit something that makes a sound. You dont even have to be very good! All you need to do is listen.

The best way to do that is to jam along with other musicians, or just by playing along to your favourite songs! Start by listening to a song and clicking your fingers. Can you click along with the beat? Can you click faster or slower along with the song?

Jamming helps you to discover what sounds you like, what notes dont work very well with certain songs, and how fast or slow songs are. It can also help you to listen to other musicians to hear when chords are changing, or maybe even what notes they are playing.
