Why Jam?

 
click here

Musicians jam for lots of different reasons. Making up music on the spot can be incredibly exciting. Jamming is also a great way to learn how to play an instrument better, and to help develop listening skills. Some skilled musicians can hear exactly what other musicians are playing without watching their hands, and can instantly jam along.

Some bands perform long jams in their concerts to make songs different and exciting at each performance. Some are even known as jam bands, where most of their songs are made up of jam sections. Other bands jam while they are rehearsing to find new ideas for songs, or to learn how their band-mates play their instruments. Because above all, playing music can be a lot of fun, and making up and jamming your own music is even more fun!
