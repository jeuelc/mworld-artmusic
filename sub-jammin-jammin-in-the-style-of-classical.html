Classical

Classical is a style of music that is very orchestrated - songs are written with lots of ideas and melodies. Improvisation used to be important, as composers would leave room for skilled players to play their own melodic solos. Composers began to carefully write these down for players, and soon classical musicians focused more on playing the music the way the composer intended it, rather than making up their own parts.

Although this is changing, classical musicians dont really jam as often as people playing other styles. But thats not to say that they cant! Classical rhythms are steady and even, and melodies use notes from the same key groups as other styles. Many classical musicians learn musical tools such as scales very carefully, and would have some excellent ideas for improvising! Classical instruments often sound great underscoring pop songs. These sounds might have been found by jamming!
