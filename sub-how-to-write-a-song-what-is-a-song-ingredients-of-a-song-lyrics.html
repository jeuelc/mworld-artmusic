Lyrics

Lyrics are poems set to music. They often rhyme, tell a story, and sometimes are a bit cryptic. The big difference is they are written so they can be sung. They really need musical accompaniment to help them come alive.

Lyrics are very important in songs that have them and most do! They help to tell the story, and often reflect what is happening in the music. Soft, slow songs may have sad lyrics, and happy lyrics may accompany fast, upbeat music. Sometimes though, songwriters might put happy lyrics to a slow, sad-sounding song to make it more complex for the listener.

Good lyrics tell a story regardless of the music though. The meaning of lyrics might be very obvious, such as retelling a story, or it might be more ambiguous. The best songs are always about something, but sometimes its up to the listener to decide what they think that is.
