Beat

 

The beat is a very important part of a song. It helps to give the song rhythm and forward motion, gives a shape to how slowly or quickly the lyrics and melody are sung, and unites them to work together with the chords. The beat dramatically defines the feel of the song too, whether it's light and fast, or heavy and slow.

Rhythm is a bit like a heart beat. A slow rhythm might help you feel at rest, while a faster beat might mimic your heart after exercising or dancing. Think of the difference between a fast hip hop song, a gentle love ballad, and a bouncing jazz song. They feel excited, relaxed or playful because of the beat.

The beat doesn't always have to be played by drums, either. It can be provided by a variety of instruments played percussively or providing a rhythmic bass line.
