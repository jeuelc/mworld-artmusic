Secrets of Art: Drawing

Drawing is almost always the first place artists begin to make artwork. We all think we know what drawing is but if we take a closer look, there is a lot more to it than meets the eye. Drawing teaches us how to look in a way weve never seen before.

We all begin by drawing from our heads. But lets look at what artists call observational drawing. Learning to draw well takes great practice. Just like playing the piano or seeing the ball when you are playing tennis.

The first thing you need to do is look at the thing you want to draw very carefully. Drawing is a kind of devotion. When you are devoted to something you look at it a lot. You really want to understand it and to draw it as it really is.

Lets explore some of the secrets of drawing.
