Cross Hatching

 

With the same set-up that you had in the earlier tone study, begin by doing a simple line drawing of your objects and the light falling on them. Rather than a solid black or dark grey, build up the hatches in these areas as in the drawing shown. You can cross hatch to create an even darker, denser tone by using layers of hatching at different angles to suggest darker tone and different textures. Of course the areas without any hatching will suggest an area of white.
