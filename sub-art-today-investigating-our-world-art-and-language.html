Art and Language

Many artists are interested in the relationship between the world and how we describe it using language.

American artist Bruce Nauman, for example, has worked in many different ways including performance, photography, film and installation. Nauman's work often has text in it. He uses humour to explore what words mean by changing the text in playful and serious ways.

Nauman often uses opposites and contradictions, and neon lighting to explore the way language can be unclear. For example in his work 'Violins Violence Silence' Nauman has confused and compared the two simple words 'Violin' and 'Silence'. Nauman's artwork shows them forwards, backwards and combined, which makes the word 'Violence'!
