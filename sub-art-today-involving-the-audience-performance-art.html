Performance Art

 

Performance art explores the relationship between the artist as performer and the audience. It's different from traditional theatre in its use of unusual and unpredictable approaches to storytelling.

Performance art can get the audience involved in a way that a piece of art that just sits there can't. Performance art is a challenge to traditional artistic practices. Usually the performance artist wants to challenge the way society works as well.

There are many important artists who have worked in this field. Perhaps the best known is the American-based artist Marina Abramovic. She describes herself as the 'Grandmother of Performance Art'.

Abramovic has produced hundreds of performances that test the limits of her body in many ways, including its endurance of pain and pleasure. Her work also tests the audience, creating situations where they are encouraged to interact with the artist.
