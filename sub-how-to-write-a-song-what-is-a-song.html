What is a Song?

Think of your favourite song. What are the parts you can remember? Is it the melody (or tune) you can sing, or the words? Maybe you remember the catchy guitar line at the beginning, or perhaps just how it makes you feel?

A song is like a short story set to music. It can be as simple as just two chords with a very basic melody and lyric. Songs often tell a story in a very short amount of time popular songs are usually around three-and-a-half minutes long.

The songs you hear today are not structured very differently from songs that were sung 100 years ago, but that doesn't mean all songs have to sound the same. Each song has unique, different characteristics. So, what makes up a song?
