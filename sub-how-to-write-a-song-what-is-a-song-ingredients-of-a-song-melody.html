The melody is a sequence of notes to which lyrics are sung, and is often the first part people hear. The melody is also called the 'top line' because it is heard on top of the chords and instruments.

Even when you can't understand the lyrics of a song, you can probably recognise the melody. It is the part of a song that you can hum, and often gets stuck in your head! Melodies are the attraction and centre of a song, and can be simple or very decorative. A good melody often takes listeners on a journey of emotion as it interacts with the rest of the song, and works with the lyrics to tell the story.
