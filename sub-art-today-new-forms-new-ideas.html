New Forms, New Ideas

Over the past hundred years artists have turned the world of art on its head! Contemporary artists have revolutionised the forms and ideas of art to reflect - and influence - your changing world.

Art isn't just drawing, painting and sculpture any more, although these are still the beginning points for many young artists. Part of the reason for the change is that artists are always questioning why the world is the way it is. Some of their ideas might work better using different materials or styles. Sometimes, though, these new ways can make people feel uncomfortable.

Contemporary artists have opened up whole new ways of looking at the world by inventing new approaches to the materials they use. They ask much more of the person looking at art. They want to draw them in, seek their involvement.
