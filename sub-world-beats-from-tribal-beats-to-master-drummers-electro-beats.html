Electro Beats

Can you imagine a type of drumming where you do not hit a skin or use sticks, and there is no drum to hit? Welcome to the world of Electro Beats, where computers and electronic instruments play any percussion sound electronically!

Electronic percussion instruments trigger (or activate) short recordings of real drums, known as samples. They include electronic drums, drum machines, samplers, MIDI devices and almost every computer music program. Some devices alter the original drum sound in slight to extreme ways, while some electronic musicians and producers have created new percussion sounds altogether.

Can you think of any songs that use electronic drum samples? You may be surprised to know that they are used in many types of recorded music, including dance and techno music as well as hip-hop, R&B, pop music and many more in fact probably many of your current favourite songs!
