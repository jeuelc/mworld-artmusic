Hip Hop is a fusion of rap, which has fast, poetic spoken lyrics, with strong beats and music made out of other song recordings. Hip hop emerged from a mixture of soul and disco music in the 1970s, as DJs used turn-tables to loop small sections of beats over which rappers then spoke lyrics. In time, sampling and beat-boxing also became important aspects of the style.

Hip hop has a strong culture, filled with dance styles such as break dancing, cultural clothing and ways of talking, and graffiti and DJs. Early hip hop music gave young people a way to express their stories and struggles of the everyday challenges they faced. A lot of these themes are still rapped about today.

Some different hip hop artists to listen to are Jurassic 5, Lauren Hill, and Will Smith.
