Let's see how you might approach making art. How about starting by drawing your bedroom? Find a pencil, a pen, or anything you can draw with, and some paper to draw on. Now think about...

Are you going to draw all of the things in your bedroom?

Do you want this drawing to be black and white or colourful?

Do you want to draw the air you breathe in your bedroom?

How about what your bedroom sounds like?

Can you draw how you feel in it?

Are you going to include any secrets in the picture?

Are you going to draw you in it, or maybe someone else?

Can you think of anything else you could add to this picture?

That's a lot of questions. You don't need to answer them all, but these are some of the things artists think about when making art.
