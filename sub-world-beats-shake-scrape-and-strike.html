Shake, Scrape and Strike!

A percussion instrument can be anything that makes a sound by being struck, scraped or shaken by the hand, sticks or beaters. Drums and percussion can make you feel happy and excited, and can also help you to make music.

Music is made up of several parts, called the elements of music. These include melody, harmony, rhythm, timbre and dynamics. While drums and percussion can use all of these, the most important musical element they use is rhythm. Rhythm plays a special role in music; it provides a feeling of motion and movement giving the sense that the song is moving forward.

In all styles of music and drumming around the world, drummers work together with other drummers and musicians to create beats.
