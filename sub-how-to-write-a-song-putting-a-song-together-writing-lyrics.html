Writing Lyrics

 

There are a lot of different ways that people write lyrics. One way is to listen to a set of chords played on a guitar or piano, and imagine the melody that would flow over them. Then, try to fit words into the melody, even if they dont make sense at first.

Another way is to write lyrics like poetry, in blocks, or stanzas of rhyming sections, and turn them into verses and choruses with music later.

Rhyming helps lyrics sound smooth and elegant. You don't have to always rhyme obvious words though, like 'love' and 'dove', or 'hat' and 'cat'. Instead, you can use words that sound similar, such as 'sea' and 'lonely', or 'home' and 'alone'. And if you're stuck, a dictionary and a thesaurus are very handy tools to find better sounding or rhyming words with the same meaning.
