Master Drummers

 

Some styles of music have only one drummer. These drummers often learn to play the drums with incredible technique, producing a whole lot of sounds. They can also learn and memorise a huge amount of very complex music, and can play long and intricate solos.

Master drummers are found all over the world in many cultures. Some of them play with sticks flying through the air, such as drum-kit players, and others play with their fingers racing over the drums, such as a tabla or a tonbak player.

Originally these instruments would only have been played in their native countries, such as India or Iran, but today you are lucky enough to be able to watch these masters and learn to play the instruments in many cities all around the world.
