Light, Dark and Shadow

Now that you have explored line drawing, you can start exploring light, dark and shadow. In drawing language, we describe this as working with tone. Once again, you have to unlearn what you think you know about tone. If you stop and really look at it carefully, tone is almost always far more interesting and stranger than you imagine.

Light is incredibly important to your experience of the world. Light enables you to see and feel things at a distance. It makes your world spacious and colourful. It makes things seem stable and ordered. Think about how you feel about your room at night compared to how you feel about it by day. At night, things become mysterious and unclear.

Darkness closes things down. It makes us feel the world is closer to us. It makes us think about touch.
