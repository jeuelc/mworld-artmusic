Choosing a Style

 

Happy songs can sometimes have bright, upbeat music, but they can also be slow and gentle. Sad songs can also be fast and energetic, or they can be made from minor chords and sound very sorrowful. Whatever you choose, the style can have a big impact on how the song is interpreted by your listener.

When you have an idea, think about the style of music that would suit it - fast, slow, energetic, bouncing, sad, quiet, noisy or loud If you like a particular style of music, like blues or rock, you might like to write in that style. Or you just might like to see what style the song sounds like in the end. A style can be changed later you could even try the same song in different styles to see which you like.
