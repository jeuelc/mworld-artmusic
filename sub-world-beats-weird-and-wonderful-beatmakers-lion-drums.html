Lion Drums

 

What sort of drum would you use to make a lion dance? A lion drum of course!

In Chinese and Japanese cultures, groups of percussionists play loud cymbals, gongs and special drums, including a big lion drum, to accompany a dancing lion.

In a lion dance, performers mimic the movements of a lion from inside a lion costume. The costume is very detailed and exciting - even its eyelashes can move!

Traditionally lion dancing was performed at important occasions such as new year celebrations, festivals, birthdays and weddings. Today, there are international lion dancing competitions held to the sound of the lion drum.
