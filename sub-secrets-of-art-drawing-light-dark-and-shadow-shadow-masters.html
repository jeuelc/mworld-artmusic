Many great artists have worked with tone to create memorable images.

Goya is one of the great painters of emotion using dramatic masses of light and shadow. Here is an image of his work Colossus, depicting a giant on the horizon. Note the way the light areas fall on the giants shoulders and face.

Seurat was a post-impressionist painter famous for his use of pointillism, tiny dots of colour that vibrated next to each other. Surprisingly, Seurats drawings used the most remarkable gradations of tone. Notice the complete absence of line in this drawing of his mother. Why don't you try to do this as well?
