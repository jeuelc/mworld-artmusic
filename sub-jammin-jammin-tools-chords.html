Chords

 
click here

Instruments that can play more than one note at a time play chords, such as the piano, guitar and ukulele. They are usually played over and over in progressions. These give basic instructions to a jamming band and a foundation for melodic solos.

There are two main types of chords - major and minor. Major chords are strong and bright sounding, while minor chords are bit darker and sadder. Playing progressions of different chords can make jams unique and interesting.

There's also another type of chord - the 'dominant 7' chord. These are moving chords that create a lot tension, meaning they dont sound very complete on their own and want to resolve. Their sound encourages you to move to another strong chord, like a major or minor.
