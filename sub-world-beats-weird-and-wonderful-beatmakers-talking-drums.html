Talking Drums

 

Did you know there are drums that talk?

By squeezing the ropes on the side of the drum under the arm, you can change the pitch of this West African talking drum to make it sound like it's talking! There are different names for this drum throughout West Africa, including Kalangu, Tamanin, Gangan, Dondo and Lunna.

We've already seen another example of a talking drum - the syllables spoken by tabla players. Do you think you could make a beat using only your mouth?
