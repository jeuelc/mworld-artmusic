Jazz

Jazz music is played with an iconic rhythm called 'swing'. Swinging music feels like it is bouncing and jumping, and makes you want to click your fingers or sway.

Jazz is a style that is full of improvisation (that's just a big word for made-up stuff!). Songs often have long sections for improvised solos, and dont have many set parts other than a basic melody and a chord progression. This means almost the whole song can become a jam. Musicians can jam along by playing chords on piano, guitar or bass, and take turns at playing a solo on these or other instruments such as saxophones, trumpet or by singing. Even the drummer might take a solo!

Jazz songs have quite complicated chord progressions that can make them difficult to play if youre just starting, but anyone can sound 'jazzier' by playing bouncing, swinging rhythms.
