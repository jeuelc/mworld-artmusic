Place a second object alongside your first object with a few centimetres separating them. Notice the space between the two objects? Think of this space as having a shape, as if the space itself was a solid thing. Is it a rectangle, a triangle or a unique shape? Look at the shapes surrounding the objects. These will form the shapes towards the edge of the page. Particularly if you have placed the objects at near eye level, you will see things in the distance that provide shapes around your chosen objects for you to draw.

Once you get the hang of this you begin to see that objects dont exist in empty space. They are in a field of relationships of other objects and spaces that are each as important as the other.
