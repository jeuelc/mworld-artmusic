A memorable example of conceptual art is English artist Damien Hirst's work 'The Physical Impossibility of Death in the Mind of Someone Living.' The work is a three-metre shark preserved in a glass tank.

What does this preserved shark have to do with the impossibility of understanding death? The combination of the shark with the title makes you pause and think. It is such a strange, threatening and beautiful creature to see close up. The mind does stop for a minute. Perhaps there are some things we just can't ever really understand.
