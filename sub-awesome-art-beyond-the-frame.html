Beyond the frame

By the mid to late twentieth century, many artists began to feel dissatisfied with traditional ways of making art. They wanted to create works that would capture their hope for a better world and even express their frustration that such hopes were taking so long to be realised.

New ways to explore the ideas of the modern world emerged. Artists today have created art that invites the viewer into the space of the art, or takes the art into spaces outside of the art gallery!
