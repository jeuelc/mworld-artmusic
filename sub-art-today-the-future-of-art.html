The Future of Art

As your world changes, so does art. Who knows what direction and forms it will take? One thing is for sure - it will be an exciting ride!

Does the world fill you with a sense of wonder? Do you feel you have something to say about your world? Do you like making things and communicating your ideas to others? Would you like to contribute your voice to this amazing field? You could be part of the future of art.

Begin by looking, drawing, making things, writing, keeping a journal, and visiting galleries... and staging your first show! Ask your friends to be involved in a group exhibition. The sky's the limit!
