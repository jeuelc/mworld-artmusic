Song Styles Showcase

There are countless different styles of music in the world of song writing, from loud and fast to gentle and slow. Songwriters like to fuse different styles to make new ones, which is why there are so many! Sometimes a style is so important that it changes everything in the world of song writing.

Some song styles are all about different sounds, but some are also about the meaning behind the style. While a good song can be played in any style, the one chosen can contribute a lot to a songs feel and emotional impact.

Most song styles are based on a number of broader, 'grandparent' styles. Lets explore some of them. Are any of the styles new to you?
