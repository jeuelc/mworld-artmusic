Lights On/Off

 

Drawing with tone is about the relationship between light and dark. It gives objects and spaces a sense of volume and solidity.

First you need to find a room where you can close the curtains or blinds. You need to be able to make the room dark.

Set up a group of your objects on a table or however you had them when you were doing your line drawing. Again, have one place from which to draw the objects a fixed point where you will remain while drawing.
