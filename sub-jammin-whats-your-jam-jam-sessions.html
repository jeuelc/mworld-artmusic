Jam Sessions

 
click here

Jam sessions are typically casual music meetings where performers come together to play music for the fun of it. Some jam sessions feature very advanced musicians who jam together in front of an audience, but many are run so that anyone who has an instrument can join in. Sometimes, a lot of musicians in a room might all jam at the same time.

Jam sessions were first started by jazz musicians, but they can be played in any musical style and with any instrument. They are popular with blues and rock musicians too, who often jam the chord progressions to well-known songs. Some jam sessions can be quite competitive, where very skilled musicians try to play better than each other! But most of the time they are fun, relaxed performances where all musicians can create music regardless of how experienced they are.
