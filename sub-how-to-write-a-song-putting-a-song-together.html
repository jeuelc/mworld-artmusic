People write songs in different ways. Some people sit at a piano and start singing sounds or random words, and make songs out of what they create. Other people have a very clear idea about the sort of song they want to write, and write lyrics based on a story of something that happened to them, a story they imagined, or just a feeling.
Some songs are written as lyrics first and then put to music. Others start as chord progressions and melodies, and the lyrics are written to fit. Sometimes whole lyrics and melodies appear almost magically in songwriters' heads it's nice when that happens!
