From Tribal Beats to Master Drummers

People play drums and percussion instruments in many different ways. Some drums are played in far-away lands by ancient tribes, while others are played in many performance halls near you.

Experienced master drummers often play very complex music alone on stage, while big percussion ensembles feature lots of drummers making enormous sounds.

And sometimes, drums are played without hitting a drum at all... Lets discover some of the different ways drumming can be performed.
