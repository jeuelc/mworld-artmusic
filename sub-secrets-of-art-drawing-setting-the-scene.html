Setting the Scene

You will need a good thick piece of paper around A2 size and a pencil, preferably a 2B pencil, and a soft eraser. Rest the paper on something solid like a book or a table top.

Once you have your paper and pencil and eraser you need to find something to draw.

Find a spot in a quiet part of the house away from distractions. Then place on this spot a favourite object. This might be a toy or anything that you would like to draw.

Next you need to find a position from which to draw the object. If you've put the object on a table this should be easy get a chair and find a spot from which to draw a fixed spot from which to make your first observational drawing.
