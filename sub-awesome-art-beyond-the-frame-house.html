House, by Rachel Whiteread

'House' is the best-known work of British artist Rachel Whiteread. This is no ordinary house, although it was once. Whiteread turns air, the invisible stuff we breathe and never really think about, into concrete!

This was a terrace house due for demolition to make way for a park. Whiteread filled every room, nook and cranny with concrete to make a monument of the space where people once lived their lives. In other words, she filled the house up with concrete! The walls and roof of the house were removed leaving the space - making the invisible visible for all to see!
