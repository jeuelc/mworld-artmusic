What Do Songs Do?

 
click here click here

Songs can make you dance, cry, laugh, think or smile! Songs are written so that people respond to them in all sorts of ways. They may have a message, or be written for a political reason. They may tell a story, or might just describe a happy feeling.

Regardless of why they were written, good songs always connect with people on an emotional level that is, they make the person listening feel something. The most effective songs have a meaning and purpose to which you, the listener, can relate. This meaning isnt always the same for each person though. Sometimes the same song can mean two completely different things to two different people. In fact, the more personally a song affects someone, the more effective that song is.
