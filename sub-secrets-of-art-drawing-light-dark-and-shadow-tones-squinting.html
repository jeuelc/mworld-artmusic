Squinting

 
click here

First, work out where the objects will be on your page. A very quick series of simple lines are enough to sort that out.

Now squint at the scene you have set up. Squinting will lower the light levels in your eyes even more. You are going to do just four tones. Where are the blacks? Where are the dark greys? Where are the light greys? Where are the whites? Notice that they are shapes of tone. Draw the shapes. Not the objects that you previously knew. Just the shapes in four tones.

This is very tricky but if you are patient and persist, you will have a wonderful drawing at the end of a few goes. Of course there are many more than four tones. In fact there is a continual gradation of tone.
