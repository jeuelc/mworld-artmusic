Cloud gate, by Anish Kapoor

Anish Kapoor is one of the most prolific and successful artists in the world today. He produces a lot of big public works such as this one, called 'Cloud Gate.' Look at the way the work includes the reflections of the earth and sky as well as those of the viewers, who are able to walk around and under this fascinating work.

Kapoor says his work is about ideas of infinity, the immeasurable and about nothing... the void! What do you think this work is about?
