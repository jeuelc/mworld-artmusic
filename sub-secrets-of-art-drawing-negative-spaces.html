Negative Space

In your next drawing you will focus on the spaces around the object. This is called drawing the 'negative space'. It reverses the thing we tend to focus on first, which is the object. Instead, you will draw the spaces between and around the objects.

This is like listening to the bass notes in a song you like. Bass notes are the deep notes, like bass guitar. Have you ever tried to listen carefully to the bass notes in a song you like?

Most people don't notice the deep notes in music but if you do, you realise they give structure to the music. It's the same with drawing negative spaces - they give structure to your drawings.
