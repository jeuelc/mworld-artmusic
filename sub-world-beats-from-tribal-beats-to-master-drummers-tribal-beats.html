Tribal Beats

 

The world of percussion is really, really old - older than the grandparents of the oldest person alive today. In fact, percussion instruments are probably the oldest instruments of them all - after the human voice that is!

Some of the oldest examples of making music using percussion include ancient tribes playing together for celebration, ritual or entertainment. Over time many percussion traditions have come and gone, just like different languages and cities have come and gone. However, there are some ancient drumming traditions that are still played in their traditional way today, such as the powerful tribal drumming of West Africa.
