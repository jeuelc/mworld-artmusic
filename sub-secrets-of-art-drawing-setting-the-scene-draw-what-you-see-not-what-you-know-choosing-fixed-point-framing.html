Secret 3 Framing

 
click here

Imagine the edges of your paper are a rectangular frame placed over your object. You might even make the shape of a rectangle with your hands and view your objects through them, imagining they are the edges of your paper. You are doing this to think about how the object that you draw will sit on your page.

For your first drawing you want the object to fill most of the page, so you need to have the top of the object near the top of the page and the bottom of the object near the bottom of the page. This sounds easier than it is and its not unusual for people to need a few goes to get this right!
