Country music came from a fusion of blues and folk, and is an enormously popular song style. It originated in Nashville, Tennessee, and is celebrated in many countries around the world. But despite how widespread the style has become, country songs are often about small, rural life in country towns. Some popular country songwriters include Garth Brooks, Adam Brand, Kasey Chambers, Dolly Parton and Keith Urban.

Country music is often played in slow lamenting ballads, or faster, sassy dance styles. Songs feature folk-style stories, and often showcase fast, impressive playing on instruments such as the fiddle, banjo, guitar and harmonica. The lyrics in country music tell stories of loss and life revelations and of real-life experiences at home and at work.
