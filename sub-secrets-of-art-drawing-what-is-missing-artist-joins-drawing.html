What else is missing in a full picture of your experience of the object and the scene you are looking at?

One thing missing is your own position within the scene that you are drawing. The objects and spaces you are drawing are connected to the space youre in, so how can you show that in your drawings?

Henri Matisse, for example, was interested in creating a perspective of feeling where he would try to dissolve the space between the world he was attempting to draw and himself.

Look at these beautiful line drawings he produced with many swirling lines like waves or rhythmic patterns. Notice at the bottom of the drawing, he has drawn his own hand drawing the drawing.
