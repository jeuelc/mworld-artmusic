What is missing in your drawing of your object and the scene you are looking at?

One thing that might be missing is your own position within the scene that you are drawing. The objects and spaces you are drawing are connected to the space you're in, so how can you show that in your drawings?

The French artist Henri Matisse, for example, often did drawings and paintings that showed himself in the drawing. For example, he would sometimes show his hand doing a drawing, as well as showing the drawing itself!

Have you put yourself in your drawing in some way? Would you like to? You could draw your hand holding the pencil, your foot resting on a chair, or your shadow on the object you're drawing. Give it a try - it might be fun!
