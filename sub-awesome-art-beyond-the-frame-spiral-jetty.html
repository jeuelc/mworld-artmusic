'Spiral Jetty' is an iconic work made by Robert Smithson. 'Spiral Jetty' is 460 metres long and used hundreds of tons of rocky material built out into an abandoned polluted salt lake in Utah in the USA.

Smithson was interested in taking art back to the environment. He wanted to join it to the grand story of natural and geological history.

The jetty has changed colour since it was built. It is now white rather than black from salt evaporating into the rocks. At times the jetty is submerged and at times it emerges again from the water. What do you think the spiral symbolises?
