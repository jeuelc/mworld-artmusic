What's Your Jam?

When musicians play a prepared song in a group, they often know how the song goes, what each musicians is going to play, how long they will play it for, and when the song will end. Jamming musicians usually dont know these things and thats what makes it exciting!

When musicians jam, they make up music on the spot by inventing new melodies and musical ideas based on what the other musicians play. To do this, musicians need to understand a little about their instrument so that when they listen to other musicians they can add notes that make the music sound good.

Musicians can jam when they are practising and performing improvised (or made up) songs or sections of music. You can hear musicians jam at concerts, and also at special events called 'jam sessions' where all of the songs played are improvised jams.
